I have watched only a few anime, but I will absolutely brag about completing *One Piece*. It is my go-to show: the thing I return to when I want adventure, ridiculous ambition, great food, found family, and a story that keeps getting bigger.

## The current watchlist

- **One Piece** — completed, endlessly rewatchable, and still my favorite.
- **Death Note**
- **The Promised Neverland**
- **Demon Slayer**
- **Jujutsu Kaisen**

This list is intentionally short. I would rather love a few shows properly than turn watching anime into a checklist.
