---
title: Reminiscing ol' times
description: A short poem on how you feel when you move into a different place to work, leaving all your friends behind.
date: 2026-07-15
tags: [Poetry]
---

⁠Living the dreams of few, while thinking about the dreams new \
⁠New dreams but still old, on how good times have gone cold \
⁠Moments reminisced on the way home, just making me feel alone. \
⁠⁠Everyday my wish grows, only to get those moments close.
