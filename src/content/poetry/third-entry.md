---
title: Falling in love again... 
description: Trying to keep track of emotions we feel through this peak-high emotion time
date: 2026-07-15
tags: [Poetry]
---

Ohh! Those feelings of uncertainty, thinking it isn't the same... \
        want to tell it with urgency, but keeping it tame... 

Ohh! How our heart always double checks, thinking she isn't the one for... \
        but our souls would melt with just a peck, and our heart opening the door... 

Ohh! What feels like clearing the fog, is the new glances & conversation starters... \
        its like the good versus bad dog, as confusing as 9 and 3 quarters... 

Ohh! What a blessing to be falling in love again, careful to not enjoy the process more... \
        for it is the love and not our intent, to actually love the person more...
