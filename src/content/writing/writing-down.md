# Hey! ✍️ This one's going to be about writing down stuff and what I have learnt from it.

Throughout my journey in college, I have faced difficult situations in which I may have had to take a crucial decision or think through something systematically. Writing gives those thoughts a ground-base: something rational to return to when everything feels noisy.

I don't enjoy writing as much as I would like. Maybe this archive is one way to keep the habit going.

## Recommendations

1. Compare thirty minutes of thinking with thirty minutes of writing and thinking.
2. Notice how writing changes your comprehension and vocabulary.
